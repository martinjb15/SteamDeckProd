Remote Play

If you already have a gaming PC, remote play is the best way to get more battery life and increased graphics on your steam deck

This can be achieved in two ways;

Steam remote play

This is setup by default, when you first turn the PC on

  1. Please make sure you pc is currently turned on and currently logged into steam
  2. Make sure the game you want to play is currently installed
  3. Turn on your steam deck and make sure you are connected to the same WIFI network as your PC
  4. Goto the library menu
  5. Click the game you want to play
  6. If the game is installed you will see a steam button if not it will ask you to install
  7. Click the stream button and enjoy
